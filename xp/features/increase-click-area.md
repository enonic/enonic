As a user, I want a more convenient way of expanding and collapsing nodes in the Content Tree so that touching the collapse/expand arrow wouldn't check/uncheck the checkbox next to it.

* Touch area around the collapse/expand arrow is big enough for a user to not have to precisely aim at it

## Dependencies

None

## Acceptance Criteria
